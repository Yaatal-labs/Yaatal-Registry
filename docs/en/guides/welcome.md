# YAATAL — The Umbrella

> **The main barrier is Access and Reach, not Creativity, Vision or Talent.**

YAATAL exists to remove West African language and dialect barriers. Anyone, literate or not, from
any community or ethnic group, should be able to express themselves freely in their mother tongue
and be heard and understood by everybody, each in their own mother tongue.

This portal is the Single Source of Truth (SSOT) for YAATAL architecture, data workflows and
registries.

## One umbrella, four pillars

| Pillar | What it is |
|---|---|
| **SDK** | the developer surface. Builders get the same reach we build for ourselves |
| **AI Models** | the sovereign model program: edge-first ASR, translation, voice and intent for Atlantic languages |
| **Commerce (BOBO)** | product lane: *be heard by the market in your tongue* |
| **Livestream / Translation** | product lane: *be understood live by everyone, each in theirs* |

One engineering principle sits beneath all four: **"models propose, the Engine disposes."** AI
suggests. Only a sovereignty-typed backend acts, and data is compiled to never leave Senegal.

## The two product lanes

### Commerce Lane (BOBO)
Voice-first market commerce. A buyer speaks Wolof or the natural Wolof-French mix to find,
negotiate and order. An on-device model proposes the action, and the Engine validates and
executes it.

**Know more:** [Commerce Lane overview](#/doc/commerce-overview) ·
[Data Pipeline, workflows & registry](#/doc/data-pipeline)

### Livestream Lane (Translation & Dubbing)
Real-time captions and audio dubbing for live broadcasts and live commerce. An auditable
moderation seam sits between understanding and speech, which is what makes live translation
deployable for institutions and public-good use.

## The shared substrate

Both lanes stand on the same foundations:

- **Yaatal-Engine**, the Rust backend: auth, commerce, payments, sovereignty types, AI cascade.
- **The data factory and review pipeline**: reproducible scripts, a source registry with
  provenance, and native human review protected by an automatic guardrail.
- **The sovereignty discipline**: license-first model choices, data residency by construction,
  and credit to the communities whose work the data rests on (GalsenAI, Soynade Research,
  Baamtu/AI4D, serge-wilson, Alwaly, Masakhane, and the upstream corpora).
