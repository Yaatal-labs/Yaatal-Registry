# YAATAL — The Umbrella

> **The main barrier is Access and Reach — not Creativity, Vision or Talent.**

YAATAL exists to **remove West African language and dialect barriers** — so that anyone, literate
or not, from any community, language or ethnic group, can **express themselves freely in their
mother tongue and be heard *and* understood by everybody — each in their own mother tongue.**

This portal is the **Single Source of Truth (SSOT)** for YAATAL's architecture, data workflows
and registries.

## One umbrella, four pillars

| Pillar | What it is |
|---|---|
| **SDK** | the developer surface — giving builders the same reach we build for ourselves |
| **AI Models** | the sovereign model program — edge-first ASR, translation, voice and intent for Atlantic languages |
| **Commerce (BOBO)** | product lane: *be heard by the market in your tongue* |
| **Livestream / Translation** | product lane: *be understood live by everyone, each in theirs* |

Beneath them all, one engineering principle: **"models propose, the Engine disposes"** — AI
suggests, only a sovereignty-typed backend acts, and data is compiled to never leave Senegal.

## The two product lanes

### Commerce Lane (BOBO)
Voice-first market commerce: a buyer speaks Wolof or Wolof-French mix to find, negotiate and
order; an on-device model proposes the action, the Engine validates and executes it.

→ In the sidebar: **Commerce Lane — Overview**, then **Data Pipeline — Workflows & Registry**.

### Livestream Lane (Translation & Dubbing)
Real-time captions and audio dubbing for live broadcasts and live commerce, with an **auditable
moderation seam** between understanding and speech — the inspectable checkpoint that makes live
translation deployable for public-good and institutional use.

→ In the sidebar: **Livestream Lane — Overview**, then the Model Inventory, the Moderation Seam,
the Duplex Atlantic Blueprint and the LiveKit/StreamSpeech analysis.

## The shared substrate

Both lanes stand on the same foundations:

- **Yaatal-Engine** — the Rust backend: auth, commerce, payments, sovereignty types, AI cascade.
- **The data factory and review pipeline** — reproducible scripts, a source registry with
  provenance, and native human review protected by an automatic guardrail.
- **The sovereignty discipline** — license-first model choices, data residency by construction,
  and credit to the communities whose work the data rests on: GalsenAI, Soynade Research,
  Baamtu/AI4D, serge-wilson, Alwaly, Masakhane, and the upstream corpora.
